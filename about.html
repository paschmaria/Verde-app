about us

who we are

Plurimus Technologies is a social enterprise that combines creative thinking, research and technology to design and develop efficient solutions to critical social problems. We look out for the intersection of a really significant problem, the best possible solution, and the most efficient technology, and work towards building and scaling those solutions, measuring our returns on investment by the level of impact we are able to make.
